Important Tips for Success

Hydration

Offer water throughout the day to keep the digestive system moving.

Key Fruits

Prioritize pear, papaya, and plum as they are naturally effective for constipation.

Food Quality

Avoid ultra-processed foods.

Safety

Adjust the texture of the food (purees vs. mashes) according to your baby's age and development.
